Speedcubing

After a long day of class in which I analyze algorithms, exercise my brain and solve problems, I like to relax and unwind by analyzing algorithms, exercising my brain and solving problems.

Rubik's cube speedsolving is one of my favourite hobbies. I have both competed in and volunteered at multiple official WCA (World Cube Association) Competitions. I like solving many different types of puzzles, but my favourite events are 3x3 and 3x3 one-handed.
